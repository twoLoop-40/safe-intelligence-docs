# Immediate Actions

## Summary

The first section of the Overview displays the TOP 5 security findings that require the most urgent attention across your organization.

## Priority Criteria

Finding priority is calculated based on the following criteria:

1. **Severity**: Critical > High > Medium > Low
2. **Asset Criticality**: Critical > High > Medium > Low
3. **Risk Score**: Severity x Asset Criticality x Weight

## Using the Table

* **Click Title**: Navigate to the finding's detail page
* **Click Domain**: Navigate to the domain's dashboard
* **Severity Badge**: Quickly identify severity by color
  * Red: Critical
  * Orange: High
  * Yellow: Medium
  * Blue: Low

## Domain Risk Map

A visual map displayed below the table that provides a quick overview of each domain's risk level. Click a domain name to navigate to that domain's detail page.

## Subdomain Risk Preview

Highlights subdomains that are favorited or have Critical findings.
