# Asset Exposure

## Summary

The second section of the Overview displays the security status of monitored domains in a card grid format.

## Domain Card

Each domain is represented as a card:

* **Security Grade Badge**: A (green) through F (red) circular badge
* **Domain Name**: Displayed in monospace font
* **Subdomain Count**: Shown as "N subdomains"
* **Severity Badges**: Critical (red) and High (orange) finding counts
* **Total Finding Count**: Displayed as a large number
* **Last Scan Time**: Shown as relative time (e.g., "5 min ago")

## Card Click

Clicking a card navigates to that domain's **Domain Detail** page. From there, you can review subdomains, findings, scan history, and more in detail.

## View All Domains

Click the **View All** link to navigate to the **Domain List** page to view all domains.

## Security Grade Criteria

| Grade | Score Range | Color | Meaning |
|-------|-----------|-------|---------|
| A | 75+ | Green | Good |
| B | 60-74 | Light Green | Fair |
| C | 40-59 | Yellow | Caution |
| D | 20-39 | Orange | At Risk |
| F | 0-19 | Red | Critical |
