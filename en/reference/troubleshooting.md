# Troubleshooting

## Login Issues

### I cannot log in

* Verify your email address and password
* Check that Caps Lock is not enabled
* If you have forgotten your password, contact your administrator

### My session expires frequently

For security purposes, you are automatically logged out after a period of inactivity. Log in again to continue.

## Data Display Issues

### Data is not displayed

* Verify that the domain is properly registered
* Wait for the initial scan to complete (up to 10 minutes)
* Try refreshing your browser

### Score is not updating

Scores are automatically recalculated after a scan completes. If a scan is in progress, please wait for it to finish.

### Subdomains are missing

Subdomains not found through automatic discovery can be added manually. Enter them directly on the Domain Detail page.

## Scan Issues

### Scan will not start

* Check if there is already a scan in progress
* Verify your network connection

### Scan is taking a long time

Scans may take longer for domains with many subdomains. Typically, scans complete within 10 minutes.

## Browser Compatibility

### Supported Browsers

* Chrome (latest version recommended)
* Firefox
* Safari
* Edge

### Recommendations

* JavaScript must be enabled
* Cookies must be allowed
* Ensure your pop-up blocker allows the SafeIntelligence domain

## Additional Support

If you have issues that cannot be resolved with the above steps, please contact your administrator.
