# Add Domain

## Registering a New Domain

Here is how to add a domain for monitoring.

### Method 1: Add from the Domain List

1. Click **Domains** in the sidebar
2. Click the **+ Add Domain** button
3. Enter the domain name (e.g., example.com)
4. Click the **Add** button

### Method 2: Auto-add via Scan

When you run a new scan and enter a domain, it is automatically registered.

1. Click **New Scan** in the sidebar
2. Enter the target domain
3. Run the scan -- the domain and its subdomains are registered automatically

## Important Notes

* Only register domains owned by your organization
* Registered domains are shared with all team members
* Subdomains for each domain are discovered automatically (powered by Subfinder)
