# Login & Account

## Login

When you access SafeIntelligence, the login screen is displayed.

![Login Screen](../assets/login.png)

1. **Email**: Enter your registered email address
2. **Password**: Enter your password
3. Click the **Sign In** button

## Signing Up via Invitation

Click the link in the invitation email sent by your administrator to navigate to the account activation page.

1. Click the **Activate Account** link in the invitation email
2. Set your password
3. Log in

## Session Expiration

For security purposes, your session will expire after a period of inactivity. You will be automatically redirected to the login screen. After logging in again, you will be returned to the previous page.

## Password Change

To change your password, please contact your administrator. (Self-service password change functionality is planned for a future release.)
