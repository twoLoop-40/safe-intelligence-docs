# Getting Started

New to SafeIntelligence? Follow this guide to get up and running quickly.

## Step 1: Log In

Log in with the email and password provided by your administrator. If you received an invitation link, click the link to activate your account.

## Step 2: Review the Overview

After logging in, the **Overview** screen is displayed. Here you can get a comprehensive view of your organization's overall security posture at a glance.

* **Immediate Actions**: The TOP 5 findings that require priority attention
* **Asset Exposure**: Security grades and finding counts by domain
* **Threat Intelligence**: Dark web exposure status and AI analysis results

## Step 3: Set Domain Scope

Click the **Active Scope** card at the top of the screen to select a specific domain. All data across every page will be filtered to that domain.

* Organization-wide view: Clear the scope to display data for all domains.
* Domain-specific view: Select a domain to show data only for that domain and its subdomains.

## Step 4: Review and Address Findings

1. Click **View All Findings** from the **Overview**
2. Apply severity filters (Critical, High, Medium, Low)
3. Click a finding to view its details
4. Update the remediation status in the **Status Change** section (Open > In Progress > Resolved)

## Step 5: Check Dark Web Monitoring

In the **Threat Intelligence** section of the Overview, click **View Dark Web Monitoring >** to review the dark web exposure status.
